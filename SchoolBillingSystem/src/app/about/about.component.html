TNPL Matric Higher Secondary School is an esteemed educational institution located in Tamil Nadu, India. It was established with the aim of providing high-quality education to students, fostering their intellectual, physical, and emotional development. The school is managed by the Tamil Nadu Newsprint and Papers Limited (TNPL), a renowned company in the state.

Our Vision

To nurture young minds with a holistic education that combines academic excellence, ethical values, and extracurricular prowess.
To be a beacon of educational excellence, producing responsible, knowledgeable, and compassionate individuals ready to contribute to society.

Our Curriculum

The school follows the matriculation syllabus laid out by the Tamil Nadu State Board, ensuring that students receive a robust and comprehensive education. The curriculum is designed to challenge students academically while also supporting their individual learning styles and needs.

Our Faculty

TNPL Matric Higher Secondary School prides itself on its team of highly qualified and dedicated teachers. The faculty members are not only experts in their respective fields but are also committed to continuous professional development and innovative teaching methods to enhance student learning.
